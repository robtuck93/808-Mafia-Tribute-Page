    • 2010: 808 Mafia was put together in 2010 when Waka Flocka came up with the idea of a production team. The team consisted of SouthSide, TM88, Purps, Tarentino, Be-Bop, Fuse, Tre Pound, DY On The Track and a few others.Video

      • 2012: The group released its instrumental mixtape in 2012, hosted by Trap-A-Holics.The mixtape was produced by multiple members. Video

      2013: In March 2013, a trailer for the tape was released on the team's YouTube channel. 808 Mafia II was released on April 23, 2013. It featured Southside, TM88, Purps, Tarentino, Fuse, Chris Fresh, OG Taxx and Be Bop.Video

        2015: 808 Mafia played a important role in producing songs for Peewee Longway, Young Dolph, Gucci Mane, Waka Flocka, Future, 2 Chainz, Meek Mill, Young Thug and many more. Video
